New Single Out

Exciting News in the World of Electronic Music: Acclaimed DJ Drops Highly Anticipated Single! In a much-awaited announcement, the renowned electronic music, "Synthwave Dreamscape," has just released their latest single, "Neon Horizons." The track, which has been generating buzz among fans and music enthusiasts alike, hit the airwaves and streaming platforms earlier today... "Neon Horizons" is a mesmerizing blend of retro-inspired synthwave elements with futuristic sounds, taking listeners on an immersive sonic journey through neon-lit cityscapes and virtual realms. The signature style, known for seamlessly fusing nostalgic '80s vibes with cutting-edge production, has once again captivated their ever-growing fan base. Early reviews of "Neon Horizons" have been overwhelmingly positive, with critics praising the track's infectious beats, ethereal synths, and expertly crafted production. Many have hailed it as a prime example of the evolving electronic music landscape, pushing the boundaries of the genre while still paying homage to its roots. The dj has also revealed that "Neon Horizons" is a sneak peek into their upcoming full-length album, which promises to be a game-changer in the world of electronic music. Fans can catch "Synthwave Dreamscape" performing live at various music festivals and events across the globe in the coming months. With the release of "Neon Horizons," their live shows are expected to be a thrilling fusion of audiovisual spectacles, bringing their cyberpunk-inspired world to life on stage. So, mark your calendars and plug into the "Neon Horizons" to experience a cutting-edge electronic sound like never before!

Road to Buenos Aires

Luke V returned to Buenos Aires with a new immersive experience. A total of 30,000 people are expected to pass through the Mandarine Park venue at the end of the three dates. It promises to be a unique show, so that your audience will have an excellent experience. The show merges computer-generated environments with aspects of real life, with the intention of blurring the boundaries between the digital and the real. For this, state-of-the-art sound is used with a giant screen that generates this 360 experience.

Our New Record Label

The well known dj, Luke V launched his own record label LV Records. In this label, they integrate various djs from various countries. The Argentinian DJ and producer says he launched his new label to pay homage to his early electronic music influences. Luke V has a vision to share “music created for iconic moments” on his new label. “With LV Records I wanted to go back to the original vision and release records that can be played at those special moments when the mood is intense and the energy is just right.” The label's first release is “Mystic,” a disco-inspired track from the Corrientes-based house music producer. He has released music on Dirtybird, Insomniac Records, HOTTRAX and more, and also runs his own label, Kolme Records.
