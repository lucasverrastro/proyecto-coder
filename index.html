Road to Buenos Aires

Luke V returned to Buenos Aires with a new immersive experience. A total of 30,000 people are expected to pass through the Mandarine Park venue at the end of the three dates. It promises to be a unique show, so that your audience will have an excellent experience. The show merges computer-generated environments with aspects of real life, with the intention of blurring the boundaries between the digital and the real. For this, state-of-the-art sound is used with a giant screen that generates this 360 experience.

Our New Record Label

The well known dj, Luke V launched his own record label LV Records. In this label, they integrate various djs from various countries. The Argentinian DJ and producer says he launched his new label to pay homage to his early electronic music influences.
